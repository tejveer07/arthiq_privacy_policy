Last Updated: November 1, 2025

Introduction
Welcome to arthIQ ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using arthIQ, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our app.

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Personal Information
When you register for arthIQ, we collect:

Name: Your full name for account personalization

Email Address: Used for account creation, authentication, and communication

Password: Encrypted and securely stored for account access

Phone Number (if provided via OTP verification): Used for account verification

1.2 Financial Data
To provide expense tracking services, we collect:

Expense Records: Title, amount, category, date, description, merchant name

Budget Information: Monthly budget limits and alert thresholds by category

Expense Templates: Saved expense templates you create

Receipts: Photos of receipts you upload (processed locally, not permanently stored)

1.3 Automatically Collected Information
Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers

Usage Data: App features used, frequency of use, interaction patterns

Log Data: IP address, access times, app crashes, and performance data

1.4 Camera and Photos
Camera Access: Used only for receipt scanning feature

Photo Processing: Receipt images are processed using ML Kit OCR to extract expense data

Storage: Receipt photos are processed in-memory and not permanently stored on our servers

2. How We Use Your Information
We use collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Core Functionality
Creating and managing your user account

Recording and organizing your expenses

Providing budget tracking and alerts

Generating expense reports and analytics

Enabling expense template functionality

2.2 AI-Powered Features
AI Chat Assistant: Your expense data is sent to Groq AI API to provide personalized financial insights and recommendations

Receipt Scanning: Google ML Kit processes receipt images locally on your device to extract expense information

2.3 Service Improvement
Analyzing app usage to improve features

Troubleshooting technical issues

Understanding user preferences and behavior patterns

2.4 Communication
Sending important account notifications

Responding to your inquiries and support requests

Providing updates about new features (with your consent)

2.5 Security
Detecting and preventing fraud or unauthorized access

Protecting against security threats

Maintaining data integrity

4.1 Technical Safeguards

Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt encryption

JWT Authentication: Secure token-based authentication system

Database Security: PostgreSQL database with restricted access and regular backups

4.2 Organizational Safeguards
Limited employee access to personal data

Regular security audits and updates

Secure development practices

4.3 Your Responsibility
Keep your password confidential

Use a strong, unique password

Log out when using shared devices

Update the app regularly for security patches

Note: While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
